Delicious and nutrious bowl recipe

This is a healthy and delicious recipe!

My girlfriend and I were looking at ways to eat healthier and we thought we should mimick the burrito bowls from Chipotle. If you use weight watchers, this delcious meal is low points!

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatos
  • 2 bell pepper (any color)
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tbs Season-All
  • 1lb of meet of your choosing (beef, chicken, or turkey work fine)
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 can of beans
  • 1 bag of califlour rice
  • 2 tsp Consumate
  • 1 tbs cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat a pan under high heat and add oil. While this is heating up prepare your veggies and make sure they are diced.
  2. Once heated, add in your veggies to the pan along with the Season-All and Cumin and cook for 10 minutes and stir them occassionally.
  3. Add your meet to the veggies and cook thoroughly.
  4. Add your beans into a pot and stir under low heat with seasoning of your choosing. When in doubt, use Season-All for everything. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add your corn to a bown and microwave for 5 minutes.
  6. Follow the instructions on the califlour and heat as recommended. When your califlour rice is done, mix in the Consumate.
  7. Once everything is ready, stir it all together and Enjoy!
